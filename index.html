Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We offer a wide range of services, including cardiology, maternity, neurology, and general health check-ups. For more details, please visit our services page.

How can I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment online through our website or by calling our customer service team. Our staff will assist you in selecting the best time and doctor for your needs.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more information about accepted providers and coverage details.

Is there parking available at your facility?

Yes, we have ample parking available for patients and visitors at our hospital. Our parking facility is safe, secure, and easily accessible.
